The Best Movie of According to Angela

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

The Big Stan

Rob Schneider is a real-estate scammer who transforms himself into a kung-fu tough guy in order to survive in prison. Once in the joint, his new skills result in a surprising turn of events--raising the ire of the corrupt warden in this raucous comedy.

Fifty First Date

"50 First Dates" is a romantic comedy where Henry Roth, a carefree veterinarian, falls for Lucy Whitmore, a woman suffering from short-term memory loss. Lucy forgets everything about the previous day, meaning Henry must win her over anew each day. Despite the challenge, Henry is determined to make Lucy fall in love with him again and again.

I Love Lizzy

"I Love Lizzy" is a 2023 Filipino romantic drama film about a young seminarian named Jeff who, while on vacation in Albay, falls in love with a local tour guide named Lizzy. Jeff's pursuit of his faith is challenged as he grapples with his feelings for Lizzy, leading to a crisis of faith. The movie explores the complexities of love, faith, and personal growth, as Jeff and Lizzy navigate their relationship amidst life's challenges.
